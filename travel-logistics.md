## Getting to South Padre Island (Start)

### Ian + wife (TBD)

Options to decide on later:

- **Drive the Tesla from Chattanooga** — ~16 hours. Would need an overnight stop (Jackson MS, Houston, or San Antonio are natural breaks). Use the Model Y for the whole tour
- **Fly in + rent a car** — fly into [Harlingen (HRL)](https://flythevalley.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Valley+International+Airport+Harlingen+TX)) or [Brownsville (BRO)](https://flybrownsville.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Brownsville+South+Padre+Island+International+Airport+TX)), rent a gas car at the airport. No EV charging to think about, can size the rental to the group

### Seattle friends — Flights in
- **Closest airports to South Padre Island:**
  - [Valley International (HRL)](https://flythevalley.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Valley+International+Airport+Harlingen+TX)), Harlingen — ~30 min drive to SPI, more flight connections
  - [Brownsville/South Padre Island International (BRO)](https://flybrownsville.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Brownsville+South+Padre+Island+International+Airport+TX)) — ~25 min drive, fewer connections
- HRL is the better bet for connections from Seattle (likely connecting through Dallas, Houston, or Denver)

## Getting Home from Destin/30A (End)

### Ian + wife
- **Drive home to Chattanooga** — ~6–7 hours via I-10 E to I-65 N. Very doable in one day

### Seattle friends — Flights out
- **Closest airports to Destin/30A:**

- VPS is smaller but closer to western 30A; ECP may have more flight options
- Both will likely require a connection (Atlanta, Dallas, or Denver hubs)
