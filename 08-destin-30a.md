## Why End Here

After 9 days of culture, history, and incredible food, the Emerald Coast is the perfect place to exhale. White sugar-sand beaches, emerald-green water, and charming small beach towns. This is "do nothing and feel great about it" territory.

## Key Spots

### [Scenic Highway 30A](https://30a.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Scenic+Highway+30A+Florida))
The 24-mile stretch of road connecting a string of planned beach communities, each with its own character:

- **[Seaside](https://seasidefl.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Seaside+Florida)) — the original New Urbanist town, famously the filming location of *The Truman Show*. Central square with food trucks, an amphitheater, and a bookshop ([Sundog Books](https://sundogbooks.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Sundog+Books+Seaside+FL))). Very walkable
- **[Rosemary Beach](https://www.rosemarybeachfl.org/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rosemary+Beach+Florida)) — more upscale, European-inspired architecture, excellent restaurants and shops. Compact and beautiful
- **[Alys Beach](https://alysbeach.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Alys+Beach+Florida)) — striking white Bermudian architecture, the most visually dramatic town on 30A
- **Grayton Beach** — the oldest community on 30A, more laid-back and artsy

### Beaches

- **[Grayton Beach State Park](https://www.floridastateparks.org/graytonbeach)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Grayton+Beach+State+Park+FL)) — consistently rated among the best beaches in America. Coastal dune lakes, nature trails, stunning sand
- **[Henderson Beach State Park](https://www.floridastateparks.org/parks-and-trails/henderson-beach-state-park)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Henderson+Beach+State+Park+Destin+FL)) — in Destin, less crowded than public beaches, gorgeous emerald water, boardwalk access
- **Seaside Beach** — right below the town, easy access

### If You Want One More Activity

- **[Destin Harbor Boardwalk](https://www.cityofdestin.com/376/Destin-Harbor-Boardwalk)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Destin+Harbor+Boardwalk+FL)) — waterfront restaurants and charter fishing boats
- **[Eden Gardens State Park](https://www.floridastateparks.org/parks-and-trails/eden-gardens-state-park)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Eden+Gardens+State+Park+Santa+Rosa+Beach+FL)) — restored antebellum mansion and gardens on Choctawhatchee Bay. Moss-draped oaks, peaceful

## Food

- **[Bud & Alley's](https://www.budandalleys.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bud+and+Alleys+Seaside+FL)) (Seaside) — rooftop bar with sunset views, excellent seafood. The signature spot on 30A
- **[The Bay](https://www.baysouthwalton.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The+Bay+Restaurant+Santa+Rosa+Beach+FL)) (Santa Rosa Beach) — waterfront on Choctawhatchee Bay, wood-fired dishes, great atmosphere
- **[Stinky's Fish Camp](https://stinkysfishcamp.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Stinkys+Fish+Camp+Santa+Rosa+Beach+FL)) (Santa Rosa Beach) — don't let the name fool you. Elevated Gulf seafood in a funky setting
- **[Cafe Thirty-A](https://cafethirtya.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cafe+Thirty-A+Seagrove+Beach+FL)) (Seagrove Beach) — upscale dining in a casual beach setting
- **[George's at Alys Beach](https://georgesatalysbeach.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Georges+at+Alys+Beach+FL)) — Mediterranean-Southern fusion, one of the best on 30A
- **[Donut Hole Bakery Cafe](https://donutholerestaurant.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Donut+Hole+Bakery+Cafe+Destin+FL)) (Destin) — famous breakfast spot, get there early

## Lodging Notes

- 30A towns have a mix of boutique hotels, vacation rentals, and inns
- **[The Pearl Hotel](https://www.thepearlrb.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The+Pearl+Hotel+Rosemary+Beach+FL)) (Rosemary Beach) — boutique, beautiful, check for destination charger
- **[WaterColor Inn](https://www.watercolorresort.com/)** ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=WaterColor+Inn+Santa+Rosa+Beach+FL)) — upscale resort between Seaside and Grayton Beach
- Vacation rental houses can be great for a group — more space and often better value
- Destin has more standard hotel/resort options if you prefer that

## Accessibility

- 30A towns are designed for walkability — flat, compact, well-maintained sidewalks
- Seaside, Rosemary Beach, and Alys Beach are all very walkable
- Beach access varies:
  - Henderson Beach State Park has boardwalk access to the beach
  - Seaside has a paved path to the beach pavilion
  - Some public access points are sand-only — check individual walkovers
- Most 30A restaurants are ground-floor with easy access

## Trip's End

This is where the driving tour ends. From here:

- **Fly home:** [Northwest Florida Beaches International Airport (ECP)](https://www.iflybeaches.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Northwest+Florida+Beaches+International+Airport+ECP)) is 30 minutes east in Panama City Beach. [Destin-Fort Walton Beach Airport (VPS)](https://flyvps.com/) ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Destin+Fort+Walton+Beach+Airport+VPS)) is 30 minutes west
- **Continue east:** If you want to keep going, Panama City, Apalachicola, and Tallahassee are all within a few hours
- **Ship the car:** Tesla can be shipped home from Destin/Fort Walton area if needed

Enjoy the sand, the water, and the view. You earned it.
